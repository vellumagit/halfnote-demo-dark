Made to fit
any occasion.

2,900 sqft of open-concept space that bends to your vision. Two kitchens, garage doors that let the outside in, and everything you need — included.

01
Two Kitchens
A full presentation kitchen plus a separate prep kitchen — for catering, cooking workshops, or food shoots.
02
Roll-up Garage Doors
Open them wide for natural light, a breezy summer feel, or easy equipment load-in.
03
Furniture Included

WiFi & Climate Control
Fast WiFi and heating throughout. Your event runs smooth, every season.
